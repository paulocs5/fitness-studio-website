Meet the team!

Welcome to our circle of passionate yoga instructors! From serene Hatha flows to high-energy Power yoga, our team brings a diverse range of expertise to guide you on your wellness journey. Whether you’re seeking balance, strength, or relaxation, our instructors combine years of experience with a deep love for yoga to help you connect mind, body, and soul. Join us as we inspire growth, harmony, and a little fun along the way.

Schedule a class!

Serene Sophia

With over 10 years of teaching experience, Sophia specializes in Hatha yoga and meditation. Her gentle, calming voice and intuitive teaching style make her classes a haven for those seeking balance and inner peace. Outside the studio, she enjoys hiking and writing poetry inspired by nature.

Zen Master Zane

Zane is a dynamic yoga instructor who blends Vinyasa flow with mindfulness practices. He believes in the transformative power of yoga to connect the body and mind. When he's not teaching, Zane is an avid traveler and finds inspiration in the cultures and traditions of the places he visits.

Harmony Hazel

Hazel is a certified Yin yoga and restorative yoga teacher known for her nurturing presence. Her classes focus on deep stretches and relaxation, perfect for those recovering from injuries or seeking a break from the fast pace of life. In her free time, Hazel enjoys gardening and holistic cooking.

Flow Guru Felix

Felix brings energy and creativity to every session, offering Power yoga classes that challenge both body and mind. A former athlete, Felix turned to yoga for healing and discovered his passion for teaching. He loves incorporating upbeat music and innovative poses into his classes.

Tranquil Tara

Tara specializes in prenatal and postnatal yoga, creating a supportive space for new and expecting mothers. With her background in physical therapy, she combines movement with education to empower women through every stage of motherhood. Tara also enjoys painting and volunteering at community wellness events.

Soulful Sage

Sage combines traditional Ashtanga yoga with modern mindfulness techniques, guiding students toward self-discovery and strength. With a decade of practice and teaching, Sage emphasizes breathwork and meditation as key components of her classes. She’s also passionate about photography and capturing the beauty of yoga in everyday life.
